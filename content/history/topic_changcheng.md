      <p>This entry is in the authoring queue. The Great Wall is not a single wall — it is dozens of overlapping defensive lines built across two millennia, by states from Qi and Yan through Qin, Han, Northern Wei, Sui, Jin, and Ming. The Ming-era wall (1368–1644) is the most photographed but is only the latest layer.</p>
